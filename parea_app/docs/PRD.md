# Product Requirement Document (PRD)

## Project Name
"Experience Companion App" (Internal Code: CO-EVENT)

## Core Mission
An interest-centric platform that matches verified users into small, safe groups (3-5 people) for real-world cultural, social, and hobbyist events, ensuring no one misses an experience due to lack of company.

## 1. Problem Statement
Many people miss out on real-world experiences (theatre, concerts, hikes, board games) because they lack available company with similar interests at the specific time. Existing solutions are either dating-focused (awkward, high pressure), ticketing-only (solves discovery, not companionship), or too generic (requires heavy lifting to organize).

## 2. Solution: Managed Social Experience Marketplace
An event-first, interest-centric platform that automates the matching of highly reliable individuals into small, robust groups (3-5 people) tailored around specific events.

## 3. Core Features & Mechanics
### 3.1. Event & Category Verticals
- **Cultural:** Theatre, Concerts, Stand-up (Paid / Verification required).
- **Social / Hobby:** Board games, Local meetups, Coffee exchange.
- **Active / Outdoor:** Hiking, Padel (Requires Tier 3 High Trust).
- **Free / Community:** Public exhibitions, City walks (Growth engine).

### 3.2. Group Dynamics
- **Group-First Default:** Groups default to 3-5 people. 1-to-1 is restricted strictly to High Trust (Tier 3) accounts.
- **Group Discount Engine:** Automatic discount (-10% to -20%) unlocks if a group fills up (4+ participants) for paid partner events, creating a strong growth loop and incentive.

### 3.3. Trust & Safety Engine
- **Identity Tiers:**
  - *Tier 1 (Explorer):* Email/Phone verified. Public free events access.
  - *Tier 2 (Confirmed):* Payment Method added or past ticket purchased. Access to standard paid events.
  - *Tier 3 (High Trust):* Identity/Selfie verified + High Reliability Score. Access to Private/Home events and outdoors.
- **Reliability Scoring System:** Internal tracking of late cancellations and no-shows. Displayed simply as badges ("Highly Reliable", "Frequent Participant") to prevent toxic person-vs-person reviews.
- **Event-Only Ratings:** Feedback is given on the event quality and the "group vibe", not standard public peer reviews. Private safety reports flag users internally.
- **Commitment Confirmation:** Mandatory check-in hold for free events to deter no-shows.

### 3.4. UX / UI Principles
- **Minimalist Aesthetic:** Clean, "airy" layout mimicking SaaS precision (Google AI Studio / Linear style).
- **Typography:** Strict hierarchy, Inter typeface, small legibility over large bold decorations, low friction. 
- **Photo-Last Strategy:** Profiles and interests lead the discovery. Photos are completely optional and only revealed to confirmed group members a few hours prior to meeting. No superficial swiping.

## 4. Monetization Strategy (Future)
- **Ticket Commission:** Integrating officially with platforms (e.g. more.com, Ticketmaster) to take affiliate cut and process group discounts inside the app.
- **Service Fees:** Group coordination micro-fee for advanced features.
- **Organizer Premium Tools:** Promoted listings and analytics for small venues/organizers.
